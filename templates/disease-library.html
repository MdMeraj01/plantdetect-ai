Powdery Mildew

Fungal

White powdery spots on leaves and stems, common in humid conditions

Affects: Roses, Cucumbers, Squash
Treatment: Neem oil, Fungicides

Leaf Spot

Bacterial

Circular brown or black spots on leaves, often with yellow halos

Affects: Tomatoes, Peppers, Trees
Treatment: Copper fungicides

Root Rot

Fungal

Roots turn brown and mushy, plant wilts despite adequate watering

Affects: Potted plants, Vegetables
Treatment: Improve drainage

Aphid Infestation

Pest

Small green/black insects on new growth, leaves curl and distort

Affects: Most plants, Roses
Treatment: Insecticidal soap

Blight

Fungal

Rapid wilting and browning of leaves, spreads quickly in wet weather

Affects: Tomatoes, Potatoes

Rust

Fungal

Orange or brown pustules on leaf undersides, leaves yellow and drop

Affects: Beans, Roses, Hollyhocks
Treatment: Sulfur-based fungicides

Disease Prevention Tips

Proper Watering

Water at the base of plants to keep leaves dry and prevent fungal growth

Adequate Spacing

Provide enough space between plants for air circulation

Crop Rotation

Rotate crops annually to prevent soil-borne diseases

Healthy Soil

Maintain soil health with compost and organic matter
