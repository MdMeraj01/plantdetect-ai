Cookie Policy

Last updated: December 2024

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you visit websites. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to website owners.

At PlantDetect, we use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience, analyze site usage, and improve our services.

2. How We Use Cookies

2.1 Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable basic functions like page navigation and access to secure areas of the website.

Examples: Session management, security, load balancing

2.2 Analytical Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting and reporting information anonymously.

Examples: Google Analytics, visitor counts, popular pages

2.3 Functional Cookies

These cookies enable the website to provide enhanced functionality and personalization based on your preferences.

Examples: Language preferences, font size choices, region settings

2.4 Performance Cookies

These cookies help us improve the performance and user experience of our website.

Examples: Page load times, error tracking, user flow optimization

4. Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services that set their own cookies to provide features like analytics, social media integration, and advertising. These third parties have their own privacy policies.

  • Google Analytics: For understanding how users interact with our site
  • Social Media Platforms: For sharing content and integration
  • CDN Providers: For faster content delivery

5. Managing Cookies

5.1 Browser Settings

You can control and manage cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • View what cookies are stored
  • Delete specific cookies or all cookies
  • Block cookies from specific sites
  • Block all third-party cookies
  • Clear all cookies when you close the browser
